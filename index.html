Brief Biography

Shunsheng Zhang is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree with College of Computer Science and Technology, Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics (NUAA) and his supervisor is Prof.Youwen Zhu . He received his master degree from Guangxi Normal University in 2021 (advised by Prof.Yuling Luo and Prof.Junxiu Liu). He worked at Guangxi Normal University from July 2021 to August 2022.
